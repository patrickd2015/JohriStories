Did you know that zebras are black with white stripes? Neither did I, but Abhishek certainly did! The sponging of such a fact can be thought of as a godlike ability to absorb knowledge, but the community surrounding Abhishek chose different vernacular to display their admiration, resulting in the culmination of Abhishek winning the “most gullible” award in the 8th grade Yearbook. And so, the people had spoken.
